Symptoms

Immediate Symptoms

• Dizziness

• Ringing in the ears

• Headache

• Confusion

• Dazed

• Fatigued

• Vomiting

• Nausea

• Amnesia especially around the time of the accident

• Unconsiousness even if temporary

• Slurred Speach

• Slow response time


Delayed Symptoms (hours-days)

• Lack of concentration

• Issues remembering stuff

• Personality changes

• Sensitive to light and noise

• Sleep disturbances

• Incorrect taste

• Depression


Symptoms in Children

• Appear Dazed

• Cranky

• Lack of intrest where they are usually intrested

• Change in sleeping or eating patterns

• Excessive crying

• Unsteady on their feet

• Easily tired
