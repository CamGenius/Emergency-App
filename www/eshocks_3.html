Electrical Shocks: Treatments

Treatments

When to Seek Emergency Medical Care

• Over 500 volts such as power lines along the road

• Noticable burn

• Unconsciousness

• Numbness

• Tingling

• Paralysis

• Vision problems

• Hearing problems

• Confusion

• Trouble breathing

• Seizures

• 20+ weeks pregnant

• Heart problems

• Muscle problems


When to Seek Medical Care

• 5+ years since victims last tetanus booster

• Burns that arn't healing

• Increasing soreness, redness, or drainage on burns

• If the victim is pregnant
