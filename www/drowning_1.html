Before CPR

• Make sure the scene is safe

• If the scene is safe and the victim is safe move to the next section

• If the victim is unsafe and you can safely remove the victim, move the victim to a safe area then move on to the next section

• If the scene is unsafe for you to retrieve the victim call 911 and wait for emergency personal to arrive

• Tap the victim on the shoulder and ask if they are okay

• If no response send one person to call 911 and another person to get and AED

• If nobody is there call 911 and get an AED if one is available

• If an AED is available use it once before starting CPR
