Drowning: CPR

CPR

• Remember to use ABC (airway, breathing, compressions) the order in which to perform cpr

• Lay the victim on their back and kneel next their shoulders

• (A) Open the victims airway by slightly lifting their chin

• (B) Check to see if the victim is steadly breathing, if not move on to compressions

• (C) Place your hands on top of eachother and press down at least 2 inches at 100-120 beats per minute

• Use the audio player below to get the beats just right

• After 30 chest compressions pinch the nose and make sure that the chin is still tilted up

• Then deliver 2 rescue breaths and watch to see if the chest rises

• If the chest doesn't rise check their throat and make sure nothing is clogging their airway

• If water starts to come up roll the person onto their side as to drain the water so that they don't keep choking on it

• Repeat the chest compressions and rescue breaths until emergency personal arrive or the person begins breathing on their own again
